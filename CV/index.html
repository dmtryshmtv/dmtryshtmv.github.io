Research Projects

2018 - present **Statistical Limits of Distributed Systems**
               Developing analytic tradeoffs between memory, data size, and accuracy
               in distributed statistical systems. In progress with J. Sharpnack.
               
          2018 **Estimating Graphlets in Large Graphs**
               Scaled the GraphletLift algorithm to estimate graphlet statistics in
               large graphs such as a 2.9M node Facebook data set. Paper submitted.
               Joint work with K. Paramonov and J. Sharpnack.
               
          2017 **Online Estimation of Power Transmission Parameters**
               Implemented and tested new sparse and low-rank regression methods
               with JuliaOpt for parameter inference on power grid simulations. Joint
               work with M. Vuffray and A. Lokhov (published in PSCC 2018).
               
   2015 - 2016 **Hidden Markov Model Inference**
               Implemented and compared inference methods for Hidden Markov
               Models. Joint work with J. Crutchfield.
