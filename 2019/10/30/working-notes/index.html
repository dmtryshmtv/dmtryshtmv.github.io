I ran some numerical simulations and obtained some complicated results. Just want to get this out there.

So a recap of the question: given a 5-ary channel, our building blocks for reasonable channels are the threshold channels ViV_iVi​, where we send a one if we see ≥i\geq i≥i ones. How do we choose this channel? Well, our heuristic currently is that the one with the maximal derivative at the fixed point maximizes the convergence rate (when I ran numerical tests to find the empirical convergence rate to an epsilon neighborhood of the iterated function, I got results that supported this heuristic).
